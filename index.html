FAQ

Q. Can I use your art as my icon/header?
A. As long as it's not a commission and/or I specified otherwise, I don't mind. However, please credit me visibly in your bio!
Q. What brushes do you use?
A. I'm always experimenting with different brushes so it changes from time to time, but for painting, I use Procreate's default Tamarr, Spectra, Styx, and Stucco brush. For the color pencil effect, I use Fatima Mandouh's color pencil brushes and Sven Hebinck's Faber Castell Polychromos pencil. For sketching/inking, I use a modified version of Procreate's Inka brush where the only change I made was changing the brush shape to a rounder shape, and the fading edge brushes from GeorgBrush.club Free Comic Ink Set.
Q. Can I reupload your artwork to another site?
A. I don't mind as long as it's not a website where I already have an account and my art is not being modified/used for AI/misused etc. However, I'd also appreciate if you could send me the link so I can confirm it's not being used maliciously.
