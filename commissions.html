I) GENERAL TERMS

  • Please be do not be rude when communicating with me, ill behavior will not be tolerated.
  • Please be clear with your request. The less references I am given, the more creative liberties I will take.
  • If you give me a deadline that I cannot complete your request by, I will turn it down. Unless I have specified otherwise, the default deadline is 3 months.
  • If I anticipate that the time will extend beyond the deadline due to (e.g. commissions I am currently working on/work/personal matters) I will notify you ASAP.
  • If you fail to follow the rules I have provided, I reserve the right to decline your commission.

II) PAYMENT

  • Payment is accepted through Ko-fi at ko-fi.com/ratfestival, or through Paypal to ratfestival.art@gmail.com
  • All pricing and accepted currency is in USD.
  • If an individual commission is over $50, payment is accepted upfront or half upfront, half after completion.
  • If an individual commission is $50 or less, payment must be upfront.

III) PROCESS

For Ko-fi Commissions Only

  • You make a tip through Ko-fi and contact me with references for your character and your preferred method of contact.
  • I create the requested piece, and reach out if I have any questions, or turn down and refund the request if I have any concerns.
  • I send the completed files for review. If there are any concerns I can make small changes, however there are no refunds at this stage.
  • The commission is complete!

All Other Commissions

  • You reach out with the details of your request. (size, polish, deadline, etc.)
  • I respond whether or not I am capable of taking your request, what I can draw for you, or suggest any simplifications to make to work within your budget.
  • If you accept, you pay either upfront or half before, and half after I've completed the commissioned piece.
  • Accept or turn down the offer (No lowballing please! I'd rather be turned down!).
  • I start working on your piece. Since I've started drawing/researching at this stage, you cannot request a refund.
  • I show you my thumbnail sketches, and you let me know what you think, and send any more reference pictures. NOTE: This is the last time to make any changes!
  • I will proceed in the direction you chose, occasionally show you my progress, and communicate with you if I have any uncertainties.
  • I'll show you the finished piece and make any small tweaks. (nothing too drastic, I can move a finger, adjust the eyebrows, or shift the colors if it's a colored piece, but if you want to change the entire pose I'd be doing 2 commissions)
  • You approve and send the other half of the payment if you sent half before.
  • I send you the completed files.
  • The commission is complete!

VI) PERMISSIONS AND COPYRIGHT

  • This is for personal use only. Unless we have agreed otherwise, I am allowed to use the artwork to promote myself with in any place or site, publish books with, to display it anywhere to my liking, and post it wherever I want.
  • Personal use does not allow for removing watermarks/artist signature, significant alterations, selling/reproducing, or any sort of commercial use of the artwork.
  • If you would like to purchase the commercial rights for printing, editing, branding, removing watermark/signature etc., please note that rights to commercial use is a 200% increase of the original price.
  • Regardless of whether the commissioner has purchased commercial rights to the piece, I retain ownership over the artwork. (e.g. you cannot claim that you or a person other than I, created the piece)
  • I do not allow my artwork to be used for NFTs. If you do so, regardless of whether or not you have purchased commercial rights to the piece, you will be forbidden from commissioning me ever again.

V) CANCELLATION AND REFUNDS

  • I have the right to cancel and refund the money for your commission at any time.
  • You have no right to cancel or demand a refund from the artist under any circumstances after full payment is received.
  • If money has not been sent yet the commissioner has the right to cancel the commission.
  • If you file a chargeback against me, your right to everything I have ever made for you will be retracted and I have the right to sell the copyrighted artwork to new buyers, you will forbidden to ever commission me ever again, and I will publicly warn others about your behavior.
  • If this scenario occurs, I also have every right to file a complaint and I have all the right to the money you have given me.

Any statement can be bound to change if discussed with me, the artist, prior to the commission.
